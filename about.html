Welcome to Quantum Lane Softworks

Where the mission is to make a difference in the gaming world.

Vision Statement

To be the industry leader in delivering technologically advanced, creatively captivating gaming experiences.



Mission Statement

Founded in 2012 to revitalize the RPG genre, Quantum Lane Softworks continues to adopt a revolutionary approach to the game industry. Through the creation of genre defining gameplay mechanics, creating deeply immersive and evolving storylines, while pushing the envelope on graphics, Quantum Lane Softworks transports players to extraordinary worlds.



Core Values

These core values embody the essence of Quantum Lane Softworks.

They form the bedrock of Quantum Lane Softworks' culture, guiding how interaction

occurs, engagement with players, and how decisions are made.

INNOVATION

Commitment to pushing the boundaries of game design and technology, creating unique and groundbreaking gaming experiences that captivate players everywhere.

MASTERY

Commitment to excellence in all aspects of game development, from storytelling and graphics to gameplay mechanics and user experience.

ADAPTABILITY

Remain agile and reactive to industry trends, player feedback, employee feedback and technological advancements to continually improve and evolve the development of games as well as company culture.

GROWTH

Committed to continued growth and advancement of employees. Strive to help employees understand their gifts fostering the ability of the individual to make a difference.

Generate personal and professional growth for employees, encouraging continuous learning and development.

INTEGRITY

Commitment to maintain the best standards of honesty and transparency in every interaction with the community and employees

NUANCED COMMUNITY

Provide a community that promotes inclusivity, and engagement between players and developers who share a passion for RPG games and support one another.

ETHICALLY CENTERED

Every action and decision are focused on sustainability and ethical practices.

Game narratives and business operations focuses on ensuring a lasting and positive impact is left on society and the environment.
