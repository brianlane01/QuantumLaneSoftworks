Importance of Developing STEM Scholars

Rob Stephenson, a National Teacher of the Year Finalist, shares his methods for incorporating STEM education into his third grade classroom. He emphasizes the importance of making STEM a core part of every child’s learning experience, not just at school, but also at home and within the community. By integrating STEM challenges into daily learning, Stephenson believes children will be better prepared for future success.
